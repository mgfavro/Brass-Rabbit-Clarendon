A Neighborhood Institution

Tucked into the heart of Clarendon, Brass Rabbit is more than a bar — it's where the neighborhood comes alive.

We serve elevated American pub fare alongside a world-class whiskey selection and cocktails that make you stay for one more round. Weekend brunch that draws a crowd. Happy hour that sets the standard.

Under fresh management and better than ever. Hop on in — we're ready for you.
